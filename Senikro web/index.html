About The Master

Meet our expert English instructor with years of experience in Toastmasters, IELTS, and English Literature. He has transformed thousands of students into confident public speakers!

Chundika Ariyawansa is a highly qualified and versatile professional dedicated to human resource development, training, and holistic well-being. Serving as our Chief Resource Person, he brings a wealth of expertise spanning management, professional communication, and psychological counseling. With a solid academic and professional foundation, Mr. Ariyawansa holds an MBA (PIM) and is a recognized member of several prestigious institutes, including AMCIPM, AMIBSL, and MAAT. As a Certified Trainer (City & Guild - UK) and a Distinguished Toastmaster (DTM - USA), he is an expert in public speaking, leadership development, and corporate training. Beyond professional coaching, he is also a passionate advocate for mental and physical wellness, qualified as a Psychological Counsellor and a Yoga Shiromani, alongside holding a Dip in English (SJVC). With his multi-disciplinary approach, he continues to empower individuals and organizations to achieve peak performance and mindfulness.
